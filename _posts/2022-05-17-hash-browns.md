For two people:

Shred about 350g of potatoes and place in a large bowl.

Add the following:

 - 1/4 cup atta flour
 - 1 spring onion, thinly sliced
 - 2 tsp nutritional yeast
 - 1/2 tsp paprika
 - 1/2 tsp salt
 - 1/4 tsp ground pepper

Mix well and form four balls. Put a thin layer of oil in large pan and
heat up a little bit.

Place the hash browns in the pan and flatten them with a spatula. Turn
over every couple of minutes until well-cooked (about 10 minutes).
