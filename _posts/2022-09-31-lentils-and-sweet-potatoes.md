That's a super easy, super delicious and super nutritious recipe!

Soak 2 cups of lentils for 30', and then wash then.

Put in a pressure cooker:

 - The washed lentils
 - Diced onions
 - One or two tomatoes, diced
 - One carrot, diced
 - Some sweet potatoes, diced
 - 1/2 tsp turmeric
 - 1 tsp chili powder
 - 2 tsp madras curry powder
 - 1/2 tsp cumin powder
 - 1 tsp coriander powder
 - 2 tsp salt
 - Water so that the ingredients are covered with water

Cook in the pressure cooker for 8'. Garnish with chopped coriander.
