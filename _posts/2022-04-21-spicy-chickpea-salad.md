In a large salad bowl, put the following:

 - 1 large cucumber, diced
 - 1 small red onion, sliced
 - 1 avocado, diced
 - About 10 cherry tomatoes, cut in half
 - 1 cup cooked chickpeas
 - 1/2 tsp red chili powder
 - 1/2 tsp fresh ginger puree
 - 3 tbsp coriander leaves, sliced
 - 2 green chilies, sliced
 - 1/4 tsp roasted cumin, ground
 - 1/4 tsp chaat masala
 - 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
 - 1/4 tsp black salt (if you have)
 - 2 tbsp lime juice (or lemon juice if you don't have limes)
 - Salt to taste

Mix well and enjoy!
