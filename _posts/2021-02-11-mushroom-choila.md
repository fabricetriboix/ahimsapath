Soak red chili in hot water

Cook mushrooms in air fryer at 180C for 10'

Cook 1 tomato in air fryer at 180C for 5'

Blend in blender:

 - Ginger
 - Garlic
 - Red chili
 - Roasted tomato
 - Little bit of timur powder

Put mushrooms and paste in big bowl

Fry methi seeds in oil, then add turmeric at the end, pour in the bowl

Add:

 - Sliced spring onions
 - Sliced green chili
 - Chopped red onion
 - Salt
 - Chili powder
 - 1/2 tsp jeera powder
 - 1/2 tsp coriander powder

Mix well
