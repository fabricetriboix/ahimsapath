Mix the following in a large bowl:

 - 180g atta flour
 - 1 tsp baking powder
 - 2 tsp brown sugar
 - 600mL soya milk
 - 1 tsp vanilla extract

Cook the crepes one by one by in a tawa or large pan, spreading the
batter using a ladle. You will need to use a bit of oil at the
beginning and between each crepe.

Eat with toppings of your choice, such as sliced bananas, melted dark
chocolate, maple syrup, etc.
