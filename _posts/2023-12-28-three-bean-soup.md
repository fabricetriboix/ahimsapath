title: Three Bean Soup
date: 2023-12-28

Soak 1/2 cup brown lentils overnight, then cook in a pressure cooker
for 1'. Drain and put aside.

Water-fry 1 onion (diced) and one garlic head (chopped) in a large
pot, then add:

 - 1 tbsp ground turmeric
 - 1 tsp ground coriander
 - 1 tsp ground cumin
 - 1/2 cup brown lentils
 - 7 cups vegetable broth
 - 1 1/2 cup red kidney beans, cooked
 - 1 1/2 cup chickpeas, cooked
 - 1 1/2 cup cannellini beans, cooked

Bring to a boil and cook for 5'. Then add:

 - 6 cups fresh spinach, chopped
 - 1 cup fresh parsley, chopped
 - 1 bunch green onions, chopped
 - 2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped (or 2 tsp dried mint)
 - 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
 - 1 tbsp madras curry power

Cook for another 5-10'.
