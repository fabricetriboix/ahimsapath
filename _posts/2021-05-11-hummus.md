This home-made version is a lot healthier than what you can buy in the
shop.

Toss in a food processor:

 - 400g cooked chickpeas
 - 3 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
 - juice of 1 lemon
 - 3 tbsp tahini

Blend. Done!
