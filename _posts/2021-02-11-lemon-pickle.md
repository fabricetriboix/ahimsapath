Cook at medium heat:

 - 1kg lemon skins
 - Ginger cut in julienne
 - water
 - 3 tbsp salt

Remove lemon skin

Add 1/2 cup sugar and 5 tbsp chili flakes

Roast & crush:

 - 1 tbsp mustard seeds
 - 1 tbsp methi seeds

Add masala to water

Cook 5'

Add 6 green chili chopped

Add back the lemon skins

Mix and put in jars
