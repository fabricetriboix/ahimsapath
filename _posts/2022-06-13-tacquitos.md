Soak 1 cup of black beans overnight.

Pre-heat the oven to 220C (with fan).

Cook 3 cups of chopped sweet potatoes, put in a large bowl.

Cook 1 cup of chopped carrots, put in the bowl with the sweet
potatoes.

Cook the beans, put in the same bowl with the carrots and sweet
potatoes.

Water fry 1 onion and 1/2 garlic head. After about 5', add the
following:
 - 1 tsp chili powder
 - a few green chilies, chopped
 - 1/2 tsp onion powder
 - 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
 - 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
 - 1/2 tsp ground white pepper

When cooked, add into the bowl. Mix and coarsly mash everything.

Take a scoope of the mixture, place on a tortilla and roll. Place the
tortilla, seam down, on a baking sheet. Repeat for the other torillas.

Bake for 10'. Eat with diced avocados, cashew cream, salsa or any
other extra you fancy.
