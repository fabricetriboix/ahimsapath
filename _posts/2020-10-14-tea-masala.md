A delicious mix of spices for black tea:

 - 20g star anise with seeds
 - 10g mace
 - 10g nutmeg
 - 10g cinnamon
 - 6g clove
 - 10g green cardamom
 - 10g black cardamon
 - 10g black pepper
 - 10g dry ginger
 - 10g white pepper

Grind the spices. I use a coffee grinder.

Add 1/4 tsp to your tea.
