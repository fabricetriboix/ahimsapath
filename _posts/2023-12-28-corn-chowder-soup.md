title: Corn Chowder Soup
date: 2023-12-28
categories: [recipe]
tags: [vegan, western, meal]
---

Water-fry 1 diced onion in a large pot, then add:

 - 4 cups vegetable broth
 - 2 celery ribs, chopped
 - 2 medium potatoes, diced
 - 1 tsp smoked paprika
 - 1 tsp dried thyme
 - 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
 - 1 lbs fresh or thawed frozen corn kernels
 - 2 tsp lemon juice
 - 1 tbsp fresh parsley, minced
 - salt to taste

Cook for about 20', until everything is cooked.

Scoop 2 cups of soup in a blender, and 1/4 cup rolled oats and blend
until smooth. Mix back with the soup.

Serve in bowls and garnish each bowl with chopped parsley, 2 tsp
nutrional yeast and optionally some hot sauce.
