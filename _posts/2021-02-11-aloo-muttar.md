Toast 1 tsp jeera seeds in a big pot.

Then add:
 - Onions
 - Ginger garlic paste
 - Tin tomato x1
 - Chili powder 2 tsp
 - Turmeric 1 tsp
 - Coriander powder 2 tsp
 - 0.5 tsp amchur
 - 4 chilis
 - Salt
 - 3 medium potatoes (sliced)
 - Add 0.5 cup water

Cook for 10' medium heat

Add 1.5 cup peas

Cook for 2-3'

Add a bit of dried fenugreek leaves
1 tsp garam masala
