This is a delicious recipe to make a healthy appetiser.

Clean a cauliflower head. Cut coarsely and place in a large bowl.

Add the following:

 - 1/3 tsp jeera (cumin) powder
 - 1/3 tsp turmeric powder
 - 1/2 tsp paprika powder
 - 1/3 tsp salt
 - 1/3 tsp chili powder
 - 1/2 tsp garlic granules

Pop in the air fryer for 15-20', or use the oven if you don't have an
air fryer.
