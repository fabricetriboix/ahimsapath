Cook 2 cup beetroot diced
Cook 3 cups of rice

In a big pot, add:
Chopped onion
Bay leaf x6
Cloves x13
Cardamom x6
Cinnamon rolls x2
2 green chili
Ginger
Garlic
1 cup tomato passata
1 tsp turmeric powder
2 tsp coriander powder
1 tsp chili powder
2 tsp garam masala
Cook until beetroot is half cooked
Add rice and mix well
Add 2 cups of coconut milk
Add water if necessary
Cook on low heat for 10'

Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and roasted cashew nuts
