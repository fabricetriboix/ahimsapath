A super delicious, super healthy, super tasty recipe! And the best way
I know to eat your Brussels sprouts!

Pre-heat the oven to 180C (with fan, 200C without).

In large bowl, mix the following in a large bowl:

 - 2 cups broccoli florets
 - 2 cups halved Brussels sprouts
 - 1 large sweet potato, peeled and diced
 - 2 cups chickpeas
 - Juice of 1 lemon
 - 2 tsp garlic powder
 - 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
 - 1 tsp ground black pepper (or to taste)

Transfer to a baking tray and bake in the over for about 1h. Give the
tray a shake from time to time.

For the dressing, mix the following in a medium bowl:

 - 1/2 cup Dijon mustard
 - 1/2 cup tahini
 - 1/2 cup water
 - 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
 - 2 tbsp maple syrup
 - 2 tbsp lemon juice
 - Salt and ground pepper to taste

Mix the dressing with the baked veggies and enjoy!
