Delicious and healthy cookies! And not just for breakfast, of course.

Soak directly in the food processor for about 1h:

 - 1 cup raisins
 - 4 tsp goji berries
 - 2 chopped dried fig
 - 1.5 cup dates
 - 1 cup soya milk

Then add:

 - 4 tsp peanut butter
 - 1 cup oats
 - 1 cup wholemeal flour
 - 2 tsp ground cinnamon
 - 3 tbsp ground flaxseeds

Blend until smooth.

Preheat oven 180C (with fan).

Make cookies, sprinkle some pumpkin seeds and bake for 12'.
