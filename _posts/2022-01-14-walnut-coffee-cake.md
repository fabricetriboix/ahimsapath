This is a vegan and healthy version of this classic, and it's super
easy to make.

Pre-heat the oven 160C with fan.

Mix in a large bowl:

 - 3 tbsp instant coffee powder, mixed with 1.5 tbsp boiling water
 - 300g atta flour (or some other wholemeal flour if you don't have atta flout)
 - 50g brown sugar
 - 1 banana, mashed
 - 300 mL soya milk
 - 100g broken walnuts
 - 1 heaped tsp baking powder
 - 2 tsp peanut butter

Mix well and pour in a baking mould.

Bake for 45' at 160C.
