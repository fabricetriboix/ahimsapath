In a bowl, add 100g frozen blueberries and 100g frozen strawberries
(or cherries, or some other berries of your liking). Defrost in the
microwave.

Then add the following:

 - Juice of 1.5 oranges (if that's too sour for you, you can use soya
   yogurt)
 - 1 tsp goji berries
 - 2 tsp raisins
 - 1 chopped date
 - 1 chopped dried fig
 - chopped fresh mint leaves

When you are ready to eat, add the following:
 - 1 tbsp ground flaxseeds
 - 1 tbsp sunflower seeds
 - 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
 - 1 chopped banana

Enjoy!
