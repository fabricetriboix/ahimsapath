Blend the following ingredients:

 - Ripe mangoes x2 
 - Vegan yogurt 250g
 - Date syrup 50g
 - Cardamom powder 1/3 tsp
 - Ice cubes: 10-12
 - Chopped cashew and pistachio 2 tbsp

You can substitute fresh mangos for frozen mango chunks.
