A baking flour mix that can be used to replace atta flour:

 - 1 cup brown rice flour
 - 3/4 white rice flour
 - 1 cup oat flour
 - 1 cup buckwheat flour (from raw buckwheat, not roasted buckwheat)
 - 1 tsp xanthan gum
