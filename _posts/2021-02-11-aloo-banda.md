Delicious curry!

Fry jeera and 4 dry red chili

Add onion

Add potatoes (long slices)

Add water

Add ginger garlic

Cook 2'

Add spices (turmeric, jeera powder, coriander powder, chili powder, paprika, garlic powder, Madras curry powder)

Salt

Add shredded cabbage

Don't add too much water

Cook until done
